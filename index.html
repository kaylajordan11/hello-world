An Easy Pumpkin Pie Recipe

Hi my name is Kayla and one of the things that I have done a lot over break is make pumpkin pies! Here is the recipe that I found, that I think tastes really good.

Prep: 5 min

Total: 3hr 10 min

Ingredients

  1. 3/4 cup of sugar
  2. 1 1/2 teaspoons of pumpkin pie spice
  3. 1/2 teaspoon of salt
  4. 1 can of (15 oz) pumpkin
  5. 1 1/4 cups of evaporated milk or half and half
  6. 2 eggs, beaten
  7. 1 pie crust
  8. Optional: whipped cream

Steps

  1. Heat oven to 425 degrees.
  2. Mix up all of the ingredients and pour it into the pie crust.
  3. Bake the pie for 15 minutes and then turn the heat down to 350 degrees and bake for an additional 40 - 50 minutes.
  4. Let it cool for 2 hours.
  5. Store in the refrigerator.
  6. Add desired amount of whipped cream to each slice and enjoy!
