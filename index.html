Easy Pumpkin Pie Recipe

Prep: 5 min Total: 3hr 10 min

Ingredients

  1. 3/4 cup sugar
  2. 1 1/2 teaspoons pumpkin pie sauce
  3. 1/2 teaspoon salt
  4. 1 can (15 oz) pumpkin
  5. 1 1/4 cups evaporated milk or half and half
  6. 2 eggs, beaten
  7. 1 pie crust
  8. whipped cream

Steps

  1. Heat oven to 425. mix all filling ingredients and pour it into the pie crust.
  2. Bake for 15 minutes then turn the heat down 350 degreese and bake for an aditional 40 - 50 minutes.
  3. Let it cool for 2 hours.
  4. Store in the refigerator.
  5. Add desired amount of whipped cream to each slice and enjoy!
